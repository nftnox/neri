Our Story

Born from a love of
flowers & Sarajevo

Nerina was born from a deep love of flowers and the timeless beauty of Sarajevo. Nestled in the heart of Baščaršija, our studio breathes life into every bouquet — each one a small poem, handwoven with care.

We work with the finest fresh local and imported blooms, crafting arrangements that last and tell a story. Whether it's an intimate birthday bouquet or a grand wedding installation, every piece carries a piece of our city's soul.

"Flowers are the music of the ground. And in Sarajevo, the music never stops."
